# Jewelry Website Design Baseline - Industry Patterns

## Competitor Analysis Summary

Analysis of 5 major jewelry websites: Rare Carat, Malabar Gold & Diamonds, Tanishq, Joyalukkas, and Kalyan Jewellers.

---

## 1. Layout & Structure Patterns

### Common Layout Architecture
- **Vertical scrolling** with modular section-based design
- **Sticky/fixed header** with logo, search, account, cart
- **Hero section** occupying full viewport width
- **Section-based content blocks** with consistent spacing
- **Multi-column product grids** (3-4 columns desktop, 2 columns tablet, 1-2 mobile)
- **Comprehensive footer** with links, social, newsletter signup

### Section Order (Typical Homepage)
1. Top announcement bar (promotions, offers)
2. Navigation header (sticky)
3. Hero banner/carousel
4. Product categories grid
5. Featured/new collections
6. Best sellers / trending products
7. Occasion-based collections (wedding, daily wear, gifting)
8. Price-range based shopping
9. Trust signals / certifications
10. Testimonials / reviews
11. Store locator / visit us
12. Newsletter signup
13. Footer with comprehensive links

### Color Pattern Summary
- **Primary**: Deep reds, burgundy, maroon (evokes luxury, tradition, auspiciousness)
- **Secondary**: Gold/warm metallic tones (reinforces jewelry positioning)
- **Backgrounds**: White or warm ivory (clean, lets products shine)
- **Text**: Dark charcoal or near-black (readability)
- **Accents**: Gold gradients for premium feel

## 3. Typography Patterns

### Common Font Choices
- **Tanishq**: Cabrito Didone (display), Proxima Nova / Nunito Sans (body)
- **Kalyan**: PTSans, Poppins (versatile modern sans-serif)
- **Joyalukkas**: Muli (multiple weights: Light, Bold, SemiBold)
- **Rare Carat**: System fonts with clean sans-serif

### Typography Pattern Summary
- **Display/Headings**: Serif or display fonts for elegance (Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond)
- **Body**: Clean sans-serif for readability (Poppins, Nunito, Inter)
- **Weights**: Light (300) for elegance, Regular (400) for body, Semi-bold (600) for CTAs
- **Hierarchy**: Clear size progression from H1 to body text

### Recommended Typography for Devi Shresta
- Display: Playfair Display or Cormorant Garamond (serif, elegant)
- Body: Poppins or Inter (modern, highly legible)
- Accent: A decorative font for special headings (optional)

## 4. Navigation Patterns

### Common Navigation Features
- **Mega-menu** system with multi-level categories
- **Search bar** prominently positioned (Tanishq makes it hero-level)
- **Category filters**: By type, metal, price range, occasion, gender
- **Visual search** capability (Tanishq)
- **Store locator** in secondary nav
- **Account/wishlist/cart** icons in header right
- **Mobile**: Hamburger menu with slide-out panel

### Category Organization
```
Shop by Category: Necklaces, Earrings, Rings, Bangles, Pendants, Chains, Bridal
Shop by Metal: Gold, Diamond, Platinum, Silver
Shop by Occasion: Wedding, Daily Wear, Office, Party, Festive
Shop by Price: Under 25K, 25K-50K, 50K-1L, 1L+
Shop by Collection: New Arrivals, Best Sellers, Trending
```

## 5. Hero Section Patterns

### Common Approaches
1. **Full-width image carousel** with promotional text overlay
2. **Split hero**: Image on one side, text + CTA on other
3. **Video background** with overlay text
4. **Search-centric** hero (Tanishq approach)

### Hero Content Elements
- High-quality lifestyle/product photography
- Headline with brand value proposition
- Subheadline with specific offer or collection
- Primary CTA button ("Shop Now", "Explore Collection")
- Secondary CTA ("Book Appointment", "View Offers")

---

## 6. CTAs & Conversion Patterns

### Primary CTAs
- "Shop Now" / "Explore" / "View Collection"
- "Book Appointment" / "Visit Store"
- "Add to Cart" / "Buy Now"
- "Try On" (virtual try-on AR feature)

### Trust & Conversion Elements
- BIS Hallmark certification badges
- "100% Certified" or "Guaranteed Purity" seals
- Exchange/buyback policy highlights
- EMI/financing options
- Free shipping thresholds
- Easy returns policy
- Customer testimonials with photos
- Store visit appointment booking
- WhatsApp chat integration
- Live gold rate display

### Email Capture
- Discount incentive ($100 off at Rare Carat, Rs 500 off at Tanishq)
- Newsletter signup in footer
- Pop-up offers after browsing time

### Exit Intent / Engagement
- Popup modals after 30-35 seconds (Kalyan)
- Category-specific popups
- Recently viewed products
- Wishlist prompts

---

## 7. Mobile Patterns

### Mobile-First Elements
- Hamburger navigation with slide panel
- Simplified category browsing
- Horizontal scroll for product cards
- Bottom navigation bar (shop, search, wishlist, cart, account)
- Tap-to-call phone integration
- WhatsApp direct message button
- Swipeable product galleries
- Sticky "Add to Cart" bar on product pages

---

## 8. Product Display Patterns

### Product Card Elements
- High-quality product image (white background)
- Product name
- Price (with strikethrough for discounts)
- Metal type and purity (22K Gold, 18K Gold)
- Weight in grams
- Quick view / wishlist button
- "New" or "Bestseller" badges

### Product Page Elements
- Multiple product images with zoom
- 360-degree view option
- Video showcase
- Detailed specifications (weight, purity, dimensions)
- Size guide
- Making charges breakdown
- Related products
- Recently viewed
- Customer reviews

---

## 9. Unique Indian Jewelry Website Features

### Cultural/Market Specific
- **Live gold rate** ticker/display
- **Gold savings schemes** (monthly investment plans)
- **Wedding planning tools** (collection builders)
- **Purity checker** / hallmark verification
- **Regional collection** categorization (South Indian, North Indian, Bengali, etc.)
- **Festival-specific** promotions (Diwali, Akshaya Tritiya, Dhanteras)
- **Auspicious timing** purchase suggestions
- **EMI/financing** calculator
- **Old gold exchange** programs
- **Gift registry** for weddings

---

## 10. Technical Patterns

### Performance
- Lazy loading for images (critical for jewelry sites with heavy imagery)
- Image optimization (WebP format, responsive sizes)
- CDN usage for media assets
- Progressive web app capabilities (Joyalukkas)

### SEO
- Schema.org markup for Organization, Product, LocalBusiness
- Location-based landing pages
- Category-specific meta descriptions
- Blog/content marketing for jewelry education

### Analytics & Tracking
- Google Analytics
- Facebook Pixel
- Google Ads remarketing
- Heatmap tools (Clarity)
